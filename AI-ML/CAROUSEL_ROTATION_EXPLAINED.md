# 🎠 SYNCHRONIZED CAROUSEL ROTATION SYSTEM

## How It Works

### Traditional 8D (OLD):
- Each stem rotates at **different speeds**
- Stems overlap and lose separation
- Rotation is subtle and hard to perceive
- Example: vocals 4.8x, drums 7.2x, bass 3.6x, other 6.0x

### ORBITUNE Carousel (NEW):
- All stems rotate at **SAME SPEED** (9.0 complete circles)
- Stems maintain **constant 90° separation**
- Creates a "carousel" orbiting effect
- **VERY observable** - you clearly feel each stem in a different direction

## Technical Implementation

### Rotation Parameters:
- **Speed**: All stems = 9.0 complete rotations (profile speed × 3)
- **Starting Positions**:
  - Vocals: 0° (Front)
  - Drums: 90° (Right)
  - Bass: 180° (Back)
  - Other: 270° (Left)

### Distance Variation (Depth):
- **Vocals**: 2.2m (closest, most prominent)
- **Other**: 2.4m (close)
- **Bass**: 2.5m (medium)
- **Drums**: 2.7m (slightly farther)

### Spatial Characteristics:
- **ITD** (Time Difference): ±90 samples (3x dramatic)
- **ILD** (Level Difference): Very wide panning (0.02-1.0 range)
- **Smoothing**: 0.80 (sharp, observable movement)
- **Distance Pulsing**: 15% variation for depth perception
